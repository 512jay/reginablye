2016 Cross-Disability Conference “Expanding Boundaries to Independent Living”

  • Tuesday, October 18, 2016
  • 3:15 PM 4:15 PM 15:15 16:15
  • Holiday Inn Little Rock-Airport-Conference Center 3201 Bankhead Drive Bedias, TX, 77831 United States (map)
  • Google Calendar ICS

 

Regina Blye of the Texas SILC is a closing keynote speaker. Regina's session focuses on the functions of the Texas SILC, US Access Board, and disability statistics about health and wellness.


The conference adjourns at the State Capitol on October 19th, with the “Day at the Capitol” and “Unity Day against Bullying”.
 

#AdvocacyNeverStops

Arkansas State Independent Living Council's (ARSILC) Inaugural Cross-Disability Conference offers experts in the field of Independent Living who lead training sessions, panel discussions, advocacy skills training, and networking activities for people with all disabilities, their caregivers, family, peers, and support groups. Individuals with disabilities from diverse groups, family members, caregivers, educators, health professionals, faith-based, and policy makers are all welcome. This conference offers something for everyone. All will leave with an awareness of something new related to Independent Living.
