I have lived in Austin, Texas for 10 years and have enjoyed the many beautiful sites this city has to offer. I have to admit, the Texas State Capitol is a site that everyone should visit when in Austin, Texas. I have had the opportunity to provide public comment at several hearings, visit legislators, and meet many advocates passionate about their issues. I decided to focus on the exterior of the building. You will see exaggerated slanted views and hard corners. Just call this my signature style, LOL!
